About Me!

I am currently a student at Harvard Extension, learning to be a full stack web developer. I am also working towards a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from Benjamin Franklin Institute of Technology. Alongside my educational endevors, I work as an intern for Seaside Sustainability, inc. This is a non-profit based in Gloucester, MA, where we work to remove plastics from the oceans, ban single-use plastics, and educate our communinity members about the effects of invasive species, pollution, and climate change.

I have experience working in many fields including design and business administration. I have a certificate in Accounting from Eii, a vocational school in Tucson, AZ. I also have an Associate's Degree in Liberal Arts from Bard College at Simon's Rock in Great Barrington, MA.

In my spare time, I am an artist and mother of a (sometimes) wonderful puppy, named Maurice. I love exploring new cities and cultures, learning more about the world and what our future holds.
